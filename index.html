Research & Publications

Revolutionizing Traditional Mouza Map Digitization Method in Bangladesh: A Paradigm Shift with Machine Learning Having Human in The Loop

June 2022- Present

Computer Vision, Image processing, Machine Learning

Vectorizing Mouza maps in Bangladesh is difficult due to their complexity. Manual digitization is time-consuming. We propose a semi-automated method that separates plot boundaries and identifiers, converting them into vector format. Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) handle pre-processing and plot detection, supported by smoothing algorithms based on map structures. Our CNN models are trained on a custom dataset. While human input is still needed for accuracy, our approach significantly outperforms manual digitization, as shown in both quantitative and qualitative results from tests on multiple maps.

  • My Contribution: Formulated the core idea, designed and implemented application.

    • Analyzing Impacts on Physiological Aspects of Rickshaw Pullers due to Heat Exposure

    June 2022 - December 2022

    Human Computer Interaction

    This poster is about the assessment of the health of rickshaw pullers using ubiquitous mobile health devices considering the impact of environmental conditions. The research aims to understand how environmental parameters affect the health of rickshaw pullers and identify potential health risks. Our study on rickshaw pullers found that heat exposure significantly affects their health. Skin temperature and perspiration increased during trips, while heart rates fluctuated, and body temperature closely followed environmental conditions. Attention levels dropped after trips due to physical stress. Many pullers worked long hours and faced heat-related health problems, with most noticing more extreme temperatures in recent years. These findings show the serious health risks rickshaw pullers face in hot conditions.

    • eDakterBari: A human-centered solution enabling online medical consultation and information dissemination for resource-constrained communities in Bangladesh

    Januray 2022

    Human Computer Interaction

    eDakterBari is a platform for providing health services to orphans, a community in Bangladesh with limited resources. The orphans in orphanages and the administrators of orphanages, and the respectable doctors are our primary users. We are concentrating on the service intermediation to provide healthcare through the suitable intermediaries for the orphans and developing interactive systems according to the user requirements. To do so, we have surveyed orphanages in different areas of Dhaka city and gathered information about the availability of healthcare services in the orphanages. Later, we deployed our eDakterBari to the orphanages with the help of intermediaries who are closely conected to the orphans in the orphanages.

    OpenGL, C++

    Compiler

    Compiler Design

    C++, Flex, Yacc

    This compiler implements essential features of modern compiler design. Lexical analysis with Flex tokenizes the source code. Syntax parsing using Bison structures these tokens according to the language grammar. An Abstract Syntax Tree (AST) is generated to represent the program structure hierarchically. Semantic analysis and type checking ensure logical consistency and type safety. The compiler then generates intermediate code for platform-independent optimizations. Code optimization techniques are applied to improve program efficiency. Finally, the compiler generates target code for x86 architecture, completing the translation from high-level language to machine code.
